# ATTR Amyloidosis Prevalence Research — Saudi Arabia

## Key Published Figure
**Source:** Mohty D et al. *Front Cardiovasc Med.* 2023 Oct 25;10:1265681. PMC10634293
- Total ATTR-CA estimated burden in Saudi Arabia: **~6,159 patients**
- Methodology: 455,222 CHF patients × 12.3% HFpEF (HEARTS registry) = 55,992 HFpEF × 11% ATTR-CA prevalence = 6,159
- Note: This is a CONSERVATIVE estimate — excludes TAVR/AS patients (adds 8–15%), gender/age weighting, and hATTR-PN patients
- Realistic total (including hATTR-PN + TAVR subgroup): ~7,000–8,500 patients

## Evidence-Based Regional ATTR-CA Estimates
Using 6,159 total × regional population proportion:
- Riyadh (27%): ~1,663 patients
- Makkah (25%): ~1,540 patients
- Eastern Province (16%): ~985 patients
- Madinah (7%): ~431 patients
- Asir (7%): ~431 patients
- Al-Qassim (5%): ~308 patients
- Jazan (5%): ~308 patients
- Tabuk (3%): ~185 patients
- Ha'il (2%): ~123 patients
- Northern Borders (1%): ~62 patients

## Important Caveats
- These are ESTIMATED figures based on population-proportional extrapolation
- No direct regional ATTR registry data exists for Saudi Arabia
- Riyadh/Eastern may be higher due to older population, better diagnostics, and referral concentration
- Actual diagnosed (treated) patients will be far lower due to underdiagnosis
- Source: Mohty et al. 2023 (PMC10634293) + GASTAT 2024 regional population data
